Welcome to JEM Hair and Beauty Studio
Serving our community since 2018 - We are a full-service salon, specializing in hair, nails and
beauty treatments.
Our team of qualified, talented and professionally-trained ladies are dedicated to your well-being
and committed to your unique and individual needs.
We are constantly expanding our knowledge and keeping up with the latest trends and the best professional
salon-quality equipment and products, to accommodate every client that walks through our doors, to provide the best
service possible.

service is delivered.

charging stations for your devices.
Our clients love that we have a loyalty program, so that they can save on the treatments they love!
With two branches conveniently located on the main road in the beautiful southern suburbs, our salon truly is a unique little "gem".
